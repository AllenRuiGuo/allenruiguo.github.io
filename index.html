Data projects

Performance of Pixc's new subscription service

  • Findings:

    • -The revenue of new subscription service was lower than prediction.
    - More than 60% of new subscriptions went into the cheapest subscription type which brought down the average price per subscription.

  • Suggestions:

    • - Try different price structures to see if it can bring 80% more subscriptions per month or raise the ratio of subscription number for the most expensive type to the cheapest type above the minimum value.

    European temperature change, situation and its consequence

  • Findings:

    • - European seasonal temperature is increasing while its atmospheric CO2 is decreasing, which indicates that a reduction in emissions does not bring an immediate reduction of temperature.
    - Little difference of temperature change between largest and least gas emission countries, which means global warming is not a regional thing, it’s a global effect and we need to make effor together.
    - The ice level in Arctic is decreasing while sea level rising. Less ice area means less reflection of sunlight, which will add more heat to the earth.
    - As the CO2 increasing, the ocean absorbs more CO2, makes the pH decreasing, which will add pressure on ocean ecosystem.

    Earthquakes in the world over time

  • Findings:

    • - Earthquake records increased since 1880s.
    - The earthquakes map kind of indicating the boundary of the earth blocks.
    - Although the higher the magnitude, the more probability to cause severe effects, the most severe earthquakes happened almost at every level, not only in high magnitudes.
    - A large number of high magnitude earthquakes happened in the so-called Ring of Fire. And also, earthquakes which caused volcano mostly happened in this area.
    - China has most earthquakes and most severe earthquakes.

    Insight of bike-share commerce

  • Findings:

    • - Bike rentals have a clear seasonal pattern. Peaks in the summer season and the lowest in the winter season.
    - There is a correlation between subscription type and the longest trip.
    - Little difference in average distance between subscribers and customers. Seasons also have little influence on the average distance of the trips.
    - Subscribers tend to use bikes to commute. Customers prefer to use bikes in the afternoon.
